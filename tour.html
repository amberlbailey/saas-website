Features

Share with other designers

This software also is provided with a discussion board. Where you and other designers can: Post projects using the fonts that have been created. Make the created font downloadable for everyone to use. Ask for feedback and give feedback to others.

Cloud Sync Service

We have icloud is built into this program. It automatically saves everytime you make a new change to your document. Plus it saves in the cloud so you can access it anywhere at anytime. You will never lose a document again.

Perfectly Match Fonts

When you realize you are having trouble trying to find the perfect match in a body copy and a heading font.We do it for you we look at your fonts created or downloaded from other designers and we generate the perfect pairing.
